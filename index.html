About Me
Most good programmers do programming not because they expect to get paid or get adulation by the public, but because it is fun to program.”
– Linus Torvalds, creator of Linux


Hey! My name's Yalzy, and as this page suggests, I'm a young, passionate website developer. Whether you're someone who knows of me via small hobby projects (such as those found here), or via someone you know who I've done work for, or even just happened to stumble upon this location, thanks for taking your time to check out my page!

It is my belief that when developing website that directly interacts with end users, one should not be satisfied by merely having a functional product, but rather when that product also boasts an easy, clear and streamlined user experience. Behind the scenes, this principle also applies, as compilers don't have a problem interpreting badly written code, however, humans do.

Outside of this, I enjoy partaking in learning in general, playing Dota2 and watching Asian dramas/Anime.
